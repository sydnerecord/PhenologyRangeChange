This repository contains code for a study that aimed to examine the simultaneous effects of climate change on plant phenology and geographic range shifts across North America, with a focus on two questions. First, are there trade-offs between phenological shifts and geographic range shifts across a broad group of taxa spanning woody to herbaceous species? Second, what is the influence of latitude, dispersal mode, and species origin (native vs. non-native) on species phenological and range responses? 

The workflow is split into three sections:
1 - Data wrangling
2 - Species distribution models (SDMs)
3 - Bayesian regression models
